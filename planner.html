🧠
Writer's Block Busters

🎯 The 2-Minute Rule

Open your doc and write for just 2 minutes. No pressure, no editing. Most times you'll keep going once you start. The hardest part is starting — remove that barrier.

🔄 Write Badly on Purpose

Give yourself permission to write terrible first drafts. Say "I'm going to write the worst paragraph ever" — this removes perfectionism pressure and often unlocks creativity.

🗣 Voice to Text

Talk through your story ideas as if explaining to a friend. Use your phone's voice memo or Google Docs voice typing. Conversation mode bypasses the "blank page" paralysis.

🌊 Change Your Environment

Write in a coffee shop, garden, or different room. Novel environments stimulate novel thinking. Even a 10-minute walk before writing sessions significantly boosts creativity.

📍 End Mid-Sentence

Stop writing mid-sentence (not at chapter ends). This gives you an easy re-entry point next session — your brain naturally wants to complete unfinished thoughts.

🛠
Recommended Tools

📝 Scrivener / Notion

Organise chapters, notes & research in one place. Better than Word for long-form writing.

🎵 Brain.fm

AI-generated focus music scientifically designed to boost deep work concentration.

⏱ Forest App

Gamified Pomodoro timer. Grow a virtual tree during focus sessions — keeps you off your phone.

🤖 Claude / ChatGPT

Brainstorm plot points, get unstuck, or develop characters. Treat it as a creative writing partner, not a ghost-writer.

  • Gentle yoga, stretching, or slow walks
  • Avoid high-intensity — your body is working hard
  • Warmth & light movement eases cramps
  • Magnesium supplements help with pain

🌱 Follicular (Days 6–13)

  • Increase intensity — try new gym classes
  • Great time for strength training
  • Energy builds daily — push yourself

Sleep Optimisation
For an overactive mind
🌡
Cool Your Room

Keep your bedroom 16–19°C. Body temperature drops during sleep onset — a cool room accelerates this. Try a fan even in winter.

📵
Screen-Free Last Hour

Blue light suppresses melatonin. Switch to a book, podcast, or journaling 60 minutes before bed. Use "Night Mode" if you must use your phone.

📝
Brain Dump Journal

For an overactive mind: write every thought, worry, or to-do in a notebook at 21:30. This "downloads" your brain so it doesn't replay thoughts at midnight.

🫁
4-7-8 Breathing

Inhale 4 counts, hold 7, exhale 8. Repeat 4×. Activates the parasympathetic nervous system and physically slows your racing thoughts.

🍵
Magnesium Glycinate

Take 200–400mg 30 min before bed. Clinically shown to improve sleep quality, reduce anxiety, and help with overactive minds. Also helps with PMS.

🕯
Wind-Down Ritual

21:00–22:30: dim lights, herbal tea (chamomile/valerian), gentle stretching or bath. The same ritual every night signals your brain that sleep is coming.

Improves mood, focus, and reduces brain inflammation. Take with breakfast. Especially helpful during luteal phase for mood stability.

🌿

Adaptogenic herb proven to reduce cortisol (stress hormone). Take in the morning for sustained calm energy. Great for high-demand weeks.
