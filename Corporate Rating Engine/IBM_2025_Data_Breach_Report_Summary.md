# IBM Cost of a Data Breach Report 2025 — Summary

**Report:** IBM / Ponemon Institute, Cost of a Data Breach Report 2025 (20th edition)
**Study Period:** March 2024 – February 2025
**Sample:** 600 breached organizations, 17 industries, 16 countries/regions
**Exchange Rate Used:** R18.00 = $1 USD (derived from report figures)

---

## Global Overview

| Metric | 2025 | 2024 | Change |
|--------|------|------|--------|
| Average breach cost (USD) | $4.44M | $4.88M | -9% |
| Mean time to identify + contain | 241 days | 258 days | -17 days |
| Recovery to full operations | 100–150+ days | — | — |
| Organizations fully recovered | 35% | 12% | +23pp |

## Cost Amplifiers (Risk Modifiers)

| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Shadow AI usage | +$670K per breach globally; +R2.2M in SA |
| Ransomware/Extortion | $5.08M average |
| Supply chain compromise | $4.91M, 267 days to resolve |
| Multi-environment data storage | $5.05M (highest of any config) |
| Breach lifecycle >200 days | Significantly higher costs |
| Malicious insider attacks | $4.92M average |

## Cost Reducers

| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Extensive AI & automation | Saves ~$1.9M ($3.62M vs $5.52M); SA: 32% lower |
| AI tools on breach lifecycle | Cuts lifecycle by 80 days |
| DevSecOps practices | Cost reduction factor |
| Data security/protection software | Cost reduction factor |
